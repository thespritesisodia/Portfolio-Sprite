Project Overview

Apna Gaaon is a web-based platform designed to bridge the digital divide in rural communities by providing essential local services online. The project focuses on empowering villagers by offering a centralized hub for accessing information, government schemes, health updates, and agricultural assistance.

Key Features

  • News Section: Local and national updates to keep villagers informed
  • Schemes Section: Information about government benefits and programs
  • Health Tips: Resources and information for rural health awareness
  • Agriculture Support: Seasonal advice and crop information for farmers
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple design optimized for low-tech familiarity

Technologies Used

HTML CSS JavaScript PHP MySQL

Impact

This platform promotes digital inclusion, helping rural residents stay informed and connected, and serves as a scalable model for rural development tech solutions. By providing easy access to essential services and information, Apna Gaaon contributes to the digital empowerment of rural communities.
