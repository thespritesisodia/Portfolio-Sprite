Overview

Apna Gaaon is a web-based platform designed to bridge the digital divide in rural communities by providing essential local services online. The project focuses on empowering villagers by offering a centralized hub for accessing information, government schemes, health updates, and agricultural assistance.

Key Features

  • News Section for local and national updates
  • Schemes Section to inform villagers about government benefits
  • Health Tips and resources for rural health awareness
  • Agriculture Support for farmers, including seasonal advice and crop info
  • Simple and user-friendly interface designed for low-tech familiarity

Technology Stack

Frontend

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript

Backend

  • PHP

Database

  • MySQL

Impact

This platform promotes digital inclusion, helping rural residents stay informed and connected, and serves as a scalable model for rural development tech solutions.
